About this site.

Greetings! This site is a culmination of work over my last semester at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock. This is a complete listing of any movie or person that has anything to do with cinema in Arkansas. All movies and people have been selected from the Encyclopedia of Arkansas.

A few things to note...

  • Some entries may be incomplete. All data is pulled from the API at The Movie Database. If there are no records from that database, some data may be missing.
  • If you see any errors with the site, or if a person or movie is missing, please reach out to me at emwilson@ualr.edu.

  • This product uses the TMDb API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDb.
